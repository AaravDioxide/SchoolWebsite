Meet the Presidential Candidates

Example Person 1 - President (WINNER)

Example Person 1 loves sports, especially football. They think the school would be a lot better if there were more football games and tournaments. They haven’t really thought of any other ideas, but they really like football. Vote for Example Person 1 if you want more football!

Example Person 2 - President

Example Person 2 is super into helping people and thinks school can be a better place if everyone is nice to each other. They don’t really know how to make everyone nice, but they promise they will try. They also like snacks and think there should be more snack breaks. Vote for Example Person 2 if you want a fun school!

Example Person 3 - President

Example Person 3 wants to make sure everyone is heard, especially those who are quiet. They don’t really have any plans, but they’re sure they can listen to people’s problems and figure something out. If you like someone who listens to you, vote for Example Person 3!

Example Person 4 - President

Example Person 4 believes school spirit is everything. They want to make school fun and full of school events like talent shows and dances. They’re not sure how it will work, but they’ll try. If you think school should be more fun, vote for Example Person 4!
